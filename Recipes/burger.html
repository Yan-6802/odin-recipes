Burger

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps to make a burger

  1. Prepare your meat
  2. Oil your grill or a cast-iron pan and grill these meat patties
  3. Prepare your vegetables
  4. Add cheese and toast your buns
  5. Throw your patties, cheese, vegetables, ketchup and mayonnasie on your buns
  6. Bite and taste the burger
